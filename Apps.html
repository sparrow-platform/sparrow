Sparrow Apps Ecosystem

"Applications ecosystem for disaster preparation, management, response and recovery"

The challenge


  • add
    Disaster victims and responders need access to accurate information
    Disaster victims and responders need latest updates and information readily available
  • add
    Information available is siloed in different apps / websites
    There are many apps and platforms that exist as point solutions - There is no one stop solution to get all data / updates.
    Accessing disaster response services requires accessing websites or downloading apps
  • add
    Disaster victims and medical workers need 'offline' digital tools
    Offlie Medical records management, Offline Notes recording, Offline translation, etc.

The Solution - Summary

Platform to host ecosystem of applets accessible through ubiquitous communication interface
Sparrow is like Alexa or Google Assistant, but way better
Sparrow is a personal assistant, which is accessible through any chat interface that exists (Sparrow Net). Sparrow doesn't just connect people to experts, but also connects people to various 'Sparrow Applets'. We created few such apps (Sparrow AI), but our vision for Sparrow platform goes way beyond!
Information needed by disaster victims and first responders can be made easily accessible through these apps.
Developers can easily create 'Sparrow Applets'
Creating new applets is simple - Create new IBM Watson assistant instances. This process is analogous to creating new 'Alexa skills'.
Existing information systems and dashboards like FEMA, American Red Cross apps, Airbnb disaster services, Task rabbit disaster services, etc can be easily integrated with Sparrow.
Developers can also create new apps for 'offline-first' scenario (linked to Mesh networks) providing various medical and psychological well-being apps for disaster victims.
Offline tools made available through Sparrow App
Sparrow App comes with offline management tools to help people affected by disasters. The App comes with 'offline' intelligence and various data management services to aid recovery during disasters.
